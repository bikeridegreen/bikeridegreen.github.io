Bike Ride Green – Track. Improve. Enjoy.

Simple, accurate cycling — with adaptive benchmarks and complete privacy.

Bike Ride Green helps you ride with precision and motivation. Track speed, elevation, calories, and effort — all tuned to your personal cycling style. Build your fitness benchmark and see your progress weekly, monthly, and yearly.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Ride Tracking — Speed, distance, time, and elevation gain in one clean dashboard. Reset max speed whenever you want.
  • Calories & Live Intensity — See how hard you’re working with adaptive intensity and calorie estimates that reflect your stats.
  • Personal Benchmarking — Adaptive metrics that learn from your rides to show real improvement (not generic averages). Optional voice updates keep you focused.
  • Smart Auto-Pause & Resume — Stops when you rest, resumes the moment you start pedaling — no taps needed.
  • Automatic Screen Management — Screen stays awake while riding and locks safely in the background to conserve battery.
  • Beautiful Ride Maps & Elevation — Full Apple Maps routes with start/finish and per-km/mi segments to visualize effort and terrain.
  • Detailed Ride History — Clear weekly, monthly, and yearly statistics for distance, speed, and calories.
  • Custom Profile Settings — Weight, bike weight, terrain type, and metric/imperial units for accurate stats.
  • Privacy First — Your rides stay on your device. Export to CSV or GPX any time for your own analysis and maps.
